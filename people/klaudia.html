Klaudia is a 1st year Master student of Astrophysics at Masaryk University, Brno. She is studying the effect of nuclear star clusters on the accretion rate of supermassive black holes found at the centre of elliptical galaxies as part of her Master thesis led by Michal Zajaček.

Her main interest lies in the high energy universe, which was the object of her Bachelor thesis. There, she focused on the AGN driven shock found in the giant elliptical galaxy M89 using the available data provided by Chandra X-ray Observatory.
